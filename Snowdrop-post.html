Snowdrop

Snowdrops are small flowering plants in the Galanthus genus.
only 4″-6″ tall, these plants have dark green grass-like leaves and dainty white flowers that hang down from the tops of the stems like teardrops. Snowdrop bulbs are planted in the fall and they bloom in very early spring (or even late winter). This lovely woodland flower is known for its ability to multiply and naturalize an area to cover the ground with gorgeous white spring blooms.
